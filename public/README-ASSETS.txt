Replace these sample assets before sharing:

1. Add your friend's photos in this folder, for example:
   photo-1.jpg
   photo-2.jpg
   photo-3.jpg
   photo-4.jpg

2. A birthday song is already included as:
   birthday-song.wav

   If you replace it with another song, update friend.song in src/main.jsx.

3. Open src/main.jsx and change the friend.name, friend.from, friend.photos, and friend.song values.

The app already works with online sample images, but personal photos will make it feel special.
